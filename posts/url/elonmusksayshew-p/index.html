Elon Musk Attending Trump Rally in Butler, Pennsylvania

Elon Musk, the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, has announced that he will attend Donald Trump's rally in Butler, Pennsylvania on Saturday. This comes after Trump vowed to return to the area where he was shot during a failed assassination attempt in July.

Details surrounding the event are scarce, but it is known that both Trump and Musk have had an increasing presence on social media and public events recently. Musk has been very critical of Democrats lately, despite previously supporting them.

The pair's relationship seems to be growing stronger as they appear together more often in person, having held a virtual interview on X (formerly Twitter) which experienced technical difficulties but is still remembered for its historical nature.
